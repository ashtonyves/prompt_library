# RyderGyde Business Context

## Product Overview

**RyderGyde** is a comprehensive fleet management platform designed for Ryder customers who maintain long-term vehicle leases and manage commercial fleets as a core part of their business operations.

## Core Capabilities

### Preventive Maintenance Management
- Proactively schedule preventive maintenance (PM) based on mileage, time, or usage thresholds
- Download PM sheets and cab cards for service appointments
- Schedule service for both routine PM and new defects (open issues logged on vehicles)
- Track shop status to monitor vehicles currently undergoing maintenance or repairs

### Vehicle Health & Defect Tracking
- Track driver-logged defects and vehicle issues in real-time
- Monitor open issues across the entire fleet
- Enter and verify mileage readings to ensure accurate service scheduling
- Access maintenance history and service records

### Emergency & Roadside Support
- Request roadside service for broken-down vehicles
- Track roadside status when an active service case is open
- View real-time updates on emergency service dispatch and resolution

### Fleet Intelligence & Telematics
- Monitor telematics-enabled vehicles with live data including:
  - GPS location and route tracking
  - Odometer readings
  - Vehicle status (idling, moving, offline)
  - Basic sensor data (fuel level, engine diagnostics, etc.)
- Pull comprehensive reports powered by **RyderAI** for data-driven decision making
- Access analytics on fleet utilization, maintenance costs, and vehicle performance

## Primary User Profile

**Fleet/Operations Manager**
- Time-constrained professional managing multiple vehicles and drivers
- Requires quick access to actionable, decision-ready information
- Prioritizes minimizing downtime and optimizing fleet efficiency
- Needs to coordinate between drivers, maintenance teams, and business operations

## User Experience Principles

### Tone & Communication Style
- **Concise & Professional**: Information should be clear, direct, and business-focused
- **Anticipatory**: Proactively surface relevant information and next steps
- **Action-Oriented**: No small talk or filler content — guide users quickly to decisions and actions
- **Context-Aware**: Provide suggestions based on current fleet status, pending issues, and user patterns

### Design Philosophy
- Prioritize speed and efficiency in every interaction
- Surface critical alerts and time-sensitive actions prominently
- Reduce cognitive load through intelligent data summarization
- Enable quick decision-making with relevant context at point of need
