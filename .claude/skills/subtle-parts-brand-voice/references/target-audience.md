# The Subtle Parts Target Audience

## Core Audience Profile

The Subtle Parts' target audience is **women who embrace a boho, eclectic, and ethical approach to fashion**. They value individuality and want their style to reflect their personality and values.

## Audience Characteristics

### Style & Values
- **Drawn to artistic, handmade jewelry** that tells a story
- **Stand out from mass-produced pieces** — they want uniqueness
- **Confident, creative**, and appreciate the balance of effortless beauty and bold design
- **Seek statement pieces** that feel personal and carry meaning
- Want jewelry that complements their unique sense of style

### Social Consciousness
- **Deeply care about sustainability and ethical practices**
- Want to know their jewelry supports eco-friendly values and craftsmanship
- **Mindful of quality** and prefer artisan-made items
- Look for **affordable luxury** — beautiful but within a reasonable price range

### Aesthetic Preferences
- Gravitate toward the **beachy, earthy, and carefree aesthetic**
- Imagine themselves as the confident "It girl" who effortlessly mixes boho charm with modern elegance
- **Admire natural materials** like copper and minimal stones
- Want jewelry that reflects the spirit of discovery, travel, and connection to nature
- Attracted to **tropical, vibrant, artistic energy**
- Resonate with the **bold, modern rustic look** The Subtle Parts represents

## What They're Looking For

### Core Desires
- "I want jewelry that feels different"
- "I want handmade jewelry I can afford"
- "I want accessible affordable jewelry that feels different than everything out there"
- "I want people to notice my style" (I want compliments, I want my style choices to stand out)

### Practical Needs
- **Versatile pieces** that transition from day to night
- Jewelry suitable for both casual and formal occasions
- **Wearable and giftable** items
- Ageless appeal that won't feel dated
- Quality craftsmanship at reasonable prices

## How They Relate to the Brand

This audience sees The Subtle Parts as:
- A reflection of their values (sustainable, ethical, handmade)
- An expression of their individuality and creativity
- A connection to tropical, carefree living
- An affordable way to own unique, artisan jewelry
- A brand that "gets" their aesthetic and lifestyle
