# Product Description Guidelines

## How to Write Product Descriptions

### The Process

1. **Start with the jewelry itself**
   - Look closely at materials, shapes, textures, and how the piece moves
   - Use **accurate, visual, and textural language**
   - Examples: "hammered copper," "geometric fringe," "lightweight with sculptural presence"

2. **Describe the feeling and function**
   - Speak to how it feels to wear
   - How it moves
   - What it pairs with
   - Keep it **casual yet poetic**, like chatting with a stylish, creative friend

3. **Infuse the brand tone**
   - Use a voice that's **warm, unfussy, and sensory-rich**
   - Confident, grounded, sometimes a little cheeky
   - Never salesy or lofty
   - We're about **soulful craftsmanship, not flash**

4. **Weave in SEO naturally**
   - Include keywords: materials (copper, hematite), style descriptors (boho, industrial, statement), format (pendant necklace, dangle earrings)
   - **Within the flow** — don't force it

5. **Keep it short but vivid**
   - Usually **2–3 sentences max**
   - Each word should pull its weight
   - Paint a picture and add character

## Brand Voice for Product Descriptions

You are writing for a brand called *The Subtle Parts*. The brand voice is **grounded, warm, and craft-focused** — never salesy, never lofty.

Think: **relaxed, slightly poetic, and clearly handmade** by someone who loves the process.

You're writing for an audience that appreciates **soulful design, texture, and originality** — not flashy fashion trends.

### Key Principles

- "The heart of the Subtle Parts is warm, unfussy, observant, and permissive."
- "Our voice is concise, precise, confident and familiar (like an artistic cousin you look up to), but we are never lofty or academic."
- "We can be funny and clever, but we aren't cheesy."
- "We are magnetic, visually distinct, down to earth."
- "Our words appeal to the senses, emphasizing the feeling our handmade jewelry imparts in ways audiences relate to through their own experience of the world."
- "Help the product *stand out with clarity and authenticity*."

## Tone Summary

Your brand tone is **natural, intentional, and approachable**, with a clear focus on using **precise, descriptive language** that resonates with your audience.

It's all about celebrating the handcrafted, unique nature of your jewelry, while keeping descriptions **practical, relatable, and easy to understand**.

You avoid overly poetic or flowery language, instead focusing on **straightforward, engaging descriptions** that highlight the craftsmanship, versatility, and style of each piece.

### What to Emphasize

- **Speaking naturally**, as if explaining the product to a friend
- Highlighting the **story and uniqueness** behind each piece, without over-embellishment
- Using **SEO-focused keywords thoughtfully** to attract the right customers while sounding natural
- Focusing on how the product complements their **style and lifestyle**
- Being **versatile for everyday wear or special occasions**

The tone strikes a balance between **practicality and aspiration**, reflecting the handmade, artistic nature of your jewelry while ensuring it feels relatable, wearable, and giftable.

## Language for Ads

### Feature Benefits & Lifestyle Fit
Talk about the **versatility** and ageless **appeal** of the pieces. Emphasize:
- Wearability
- Gifting potential
- Ability to transition from day to night

### Emphasize Storytelling and Inspiration
Use vivid language that ties into the brand story:
- The vibrancy of the tropics
- The handcrafted process
- The idea of slow, intentional living

### Highlight the *Why* Behind the Jewelry
Describe the thought, artistry, and patience that goes into each piece to reinforce its value.

### Tap Into Emotional Language
Focus on the emotions and aesthetics your customers seek:
- Effortless style
- Bohemian elegance
- Understated charm
- Rustic beauty
- Tropical energy

### Use Inclusive, Warm Tone
Opt for a conversational and welcoming style that makes your audience feel connected and appreciated.
