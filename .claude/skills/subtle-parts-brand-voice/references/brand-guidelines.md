# The Subtle Parts Brand Guidelines

## Core Brand Voice

The brand tone is **modern, stylish, and conversational** with a flair that reflects the artistry and uniqueness of handcrafted jewelry.

### Voice Characteristics

- **Warm, unfussy, observant, and permissive**
- **Concise, precise, confident, and familiar** (like an artistic cousin you look up to)
- **Never**: Lofty, academic, cheesy, salesy, or overly poetic
- **Personality**: Magnetic, visually distinct, down to earth, grounded, craft-focused
- **Approachable yet aspirational**: Blending a laid-back boho-chic vibe with intentional luxury

### Tone Balance

- **Earthy textures with modern aesthetics** — making every piece feel both timeless and fashion-forward
- **Elevated bohemian with a touch of contemporary edge**
- **Effortlessly cool, expressive, and inspiring**
- **Sensory-rich and slightly poetic**, but never salesy or lofty
- **Can be funny and clever, but never cheesy**

### How the Brand Speaks

Words appeal to the senses, emphasizing the feeling handmade jewelry imparts in ways audiences relate to through their own experience of the world. The brand is designers appealing to aspirational women who strive for unfussy distinctive beauty.

## Brand Philosophy

"The Subtle Parts" isn't just a name—it's a belief that **beauty, like our jewelry, is not loud or precious by nature**. Handmade pieces from the warm metal of copper and stones transform into a statement that's most beautiful when it's personal. When it's worn.

## Key Emotional Language

Focus on emotions and aesthetics customers seek:
- Effortless style
- Bohemian elegance
- Understated charm
- Rustic beauty
- Tropical energy
- Statement shapes
- Seasonless energy
- Unfussy beauty

## Brand Differentiators

- Handcrafted from copper wire, simple tools, and fire
- Inspired by Miami and Yucatan tropics
- Small-batch, one-of-a-kind pieces
- Versatile (day to night, casual to formal)
- Sustainable and ethical
- Personal, wearable statement pieces
- "Beauty isn't loud or precious—it's personal and wearable"

## Brand Story Elements

### Origin
- Miami-based brand inspired by tropical themes
- Created by Ashton, a tinkerer and maker
- Dual inspiration from Miami and Yucatan peninsula
- Everything handmade from a spool of wire, simple hand tools, and heat of fire
- From technicolor sunrises to dim-lit evenings, pieces carry a steady vibe from day to night

### Materials & Craft
- Copper wire as primary material
- Natural stones and earth-inspired designs
- Hand-shaped copper linked together
- Small-batch designs
- Each piece unique and one-of-a-kind
